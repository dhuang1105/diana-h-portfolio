About Me

Hi! Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Diana Huang, and I am a sophomore Computer Science and Engineering major at Santa Clara University. I created this portfolio to bring together my passions for coding, photography, and Ultimate Frisbee. Photography allows me to freeze moments in time — whether it's the intensity of a close game, or the joy on player’s faces after a big win. This site is a space for me to share the way I see the world — through both the lens of a camera and the logic of code. Explore my work, and if something speaks to you, let’s connect!
